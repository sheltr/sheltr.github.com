Sheltr is a web and mobile-friendly platform to locate shelters and services for the homeless. It is designed for service providers for the homeless and the general public.

View philly.sheltr.org, the first deployment of Sheltr.

Sheltr was created at RHoK Philly, December 3rd and 4th, 2011.
